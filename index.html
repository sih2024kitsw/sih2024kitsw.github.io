Registration Process

  • Assemble your team

  • Select a problem statement from the SIH Website


  • Download the Hardcopy of the Registration Form


  • Fill the form with relevant details and pay the registration fee of Rs.300 to Registration Committee (UPI Not Accepted)


  • Fill the Google Form with the relevant details as written in the hard copy and provide proof of payment


  • Submit the hardcopy to the Registration Committee


  • Download the Idea Presentation PPT and pitch your idea on 31st August!

Team Requirements

A Maximum of 6 Members in the team
A Minimum of 2 Girl Members in the team
A Professor as a Team Mentor from any department in our college
